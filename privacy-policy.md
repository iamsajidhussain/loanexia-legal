## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 22 January 2026**

Loanexia respects your privacy and is designed to work with **minimal data collection**.
This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and what is **not** collected.

---

## Overview

Loanexia is a loan comparison and decision-support application.
It helps users understand the **total financial impact of loans**, including EMI, interest, and total payable.

Loanexia does **not** sell loans, recommend lenders, or promote financial products.

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Loanexia does **not** collect or store:

* Personal identity information (name, phone number, email, address)
* Bank account details
* Aadhaar, PAN, or any government identifiers
* Credit scores or credit reports
* Login credentials (there is no user account system)
* Contact lists or device files
* Location data

You can use the app without creating an account.

---

## Information You Enter in the App

Any loan details you enter (such as loan amount, interest rate, or tenure) are:

* Entered voluntarily by you
* Used **only** to perform calculations on your device
* Not transmitted to our servers
* Not shared with any third party

These inputs exist solely to show calculation results to you.

---

## Calculations and Processing

All calculations in Loanexia use standard, deterministic amortization formulas.

* No external APIs are used for calculations
* No hidden manipulation or ranking logic exists
* The same inputs always produce the same outputs

Loanexia does not alter results based on user behavior or preferences.

---

## Data Storage

* Loan scenarios may be stored **locally on your device** to support app functionality (such as saved comparisons)
* This data remains on your device unless you remove the app or clear app data
* Loanexia does not maintain a central user database

---

## Analytics and Tracking

Loanexia does **not** use:

* Advertising trackers
* Behavioral profiling
* Third-party analytics SDKs that track users across apps

If basic, anonymous usage metrics are introduced in the future, this policy will be updated clearly.

---

## Third-Party Services

Loanexia does not share your data with lenders, advertisers, or marketing partners.

Links to external platforms (such as app stores or social platforms) are optional and user-initiated.
Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Loanexia is not intended for use by children under 18.

We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

---

## Your Responsibility

Loanexia is a **decision-support tool**.

Actual loan terms, fees, and conditions may vary by lender.
Users should verify all loan details directly with their chosen lender before making financial commitments.

---
